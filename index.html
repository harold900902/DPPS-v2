What we are

DePeuter Packaging Solutions (DPPS) is a provider of automated material handling solutions, fully integrated or stand alone. Our solutions include, but are not limited to, the following equipment: palletizers, de-palletizers, robotic material handling, pick and place, case packaging machines, conveyor systems, and totally integrated factory automation.

We count on professional engineers capable to program diverse automation controllers including PLCs (Programmable Logic Controllers), PACs (Programmable Automation Controllers) and their corresponding interfaces (HMIs). We program and configure automation equipment including motion controllers (motors, servos), robots, machine vision cameras, and other measurement equipment.

Why to choose DPPS

DPPS supplies anything from small single cell robotic projects to turnkey, totally integrated factory automation systems. All our systems are outfitted with Yaskawa Motoman or Fanuc Robots to ensure the latest requirements in regard to safety; and can be integrated to our INFINITE™ control system, that combines all system components into our web-based control and monitoring system. Providing identity inside the Industry 4.0

Many offerings you find out in the field are “off the shelf”, universal software and hardware solutions for current Industry 4.0 offerings. Ours is a custom solution, that allow our customers to log in through a personalized portal. And once inside the web pages, it will show the product and data specific to this client. This log in can be local, at the machinery, just at the plant level, or on the world wide web. The system can also replace your current PLC CPU and HMI screens, and on the same platform that handles the data transfers, run your local controls, providing for additional reduction in capital cost when building and designing tools for the future.
